Friends of Guimaras

Supporting people. Building futures.

A small community-driven initiative supporting people and local projects in Guimaras, Philippines.

Support our work

Real friendship. Real support.

Friends of Guimaras is a volunteer initiative built on friendship, compassion and a shared wish to support people and communities in Guimaras, Philippines.

The work is mainly led by Igor, Phil and Hartmut. Friends and supporters contribute through donations, visits, materials, practical work and encouragement.

Our projects

Food & daily support

Helping with food, basic needs and practical support for families and people who need encouragement and assistance.

Medical help

Supporting urgent medical needs and helping people access basic care where possible.

Housing & community help

Supporting practical local projects that improve living conditions step by step.

Education & opportunity

Helping children, youth and families with small but meaningful support that can open new opportunities.
